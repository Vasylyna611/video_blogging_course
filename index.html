About Us

Our video blogging school has been working for 3 years. We have gathered the best practicing video bloggers and influencers who know everything about YouTube, Instagram, and other media platforms. And they are ready to share their knowledge with you! We offer vlogging courses for those who are ready to throw off their shyness and start vlogging like a pro.

Video Production

You won't just learn how to talk in front of the camera. You will learn the basics of video production and be able to direct and shoot vlogs yourself.

Online Courses

All our courses are available online for users from all over the world. We accompany all lectures with subtitles. You can also watch recorded webinars.

Workshops

All lectures are accompanied by workshops. You will learn directing, correct diction, etc. A lot of homework awaits you!

Portfolio

At the end of the course, you will get a ready portfolio. If you decide to do video blogging as a director or cameraman, this portfolio will come in handy.
